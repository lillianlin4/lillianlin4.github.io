Photo Manipulation Station Brand Design

The Photo Manipulation Station is one of many companies designed by students in a design course. It was assigned to me as an academic branding project that focused on logo creation, stationery design, layout design, and packaging or apparel design.

The brand focuses on novelty photo services targeting younger audiences. They provide photo editing and photoshoot services for fans who want to see themselves in their favorite works.

To fit the brand image of fun, friendly, nerdy, and imaginative, the logo incorporates a baby dragon that acts as a mascot. Rather than a more abstract and simple logo, it better establishes a personal connection, especially for children. In order to appeal to a wider range of audiences, I focused on a more general fantasy theme rather than specific shows, movies, games, or comics. Characters and graphics are designed to with the same color theme.
