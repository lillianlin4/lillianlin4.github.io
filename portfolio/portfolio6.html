M.A.P. Shoe Projector Graphics

M.A.P. is a brand under Vida Shoes International, a company dedicated to designing, producing and marketing quality branded footwear. Durable and stylish, these shoes are designed to provide comfort and protection in the outdoor environment.

These instructions and templates were designed for Vida Kids, a division of Vida Shoes International, as graphics for the D.I.Y shoebox projector project. Instructions and graphics are designed for easy understanding while adhering to branding guidelines.
