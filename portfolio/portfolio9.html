Sleep Information Design Page and Spread

This academic project explored information design and the use of graphic design to better communicate data and information in scientific and mathematical subjects.

Based on graphs and information from sleep research and experiments, I designed a National Geographic Magazine page on the stages of sleep. The content is later expanded in the spread version with additional information and graphs, emphasizing on REM sleep.
