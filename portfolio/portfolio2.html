Electronic Gaming Magazine Redesign

The Electronic Gaming Magazine is a monthly publication about video game news, industry events coverage, product reviews, interviews, and editorial content.

For this academic project, I redesigned the masthead, cover page, and a spread, focusing on research, imagery, grid system, layout, for a dynamic new look. The design of the masthead is inspired by video game controllers, with the shape G's actings as analog sticks.
