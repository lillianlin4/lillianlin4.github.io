Energy Interactive Guide

This academic project explored information design and the use of graphic design to better communicate data and information in scientific and mathematical subjects.

The interactive is mainly based on an energy graph about the amount of energy it takes to power a 100-watt light bulb for a year for various energy sources. With additional research, relevant information is added to the guide. The design is striped down to its content and icons, emphasizing on simplicity and organization for easy reading and comprehension.
