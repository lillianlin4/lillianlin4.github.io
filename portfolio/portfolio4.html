Dive Into Difference Week Campaign

Dive into Difference Week was a program held by the Residential Assistants of College-In-The-Woods (CIW) residential community in Binghamton University. Events took place from September 28th to October 2nd and focused on promoting diversity on campus. In order to reach as many CIW residents as possible and to have consistent advertising, they required advertising for the entire program along with advertising for the individual events by the Residential Assistants.
