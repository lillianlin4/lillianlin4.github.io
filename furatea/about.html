About

We are an international teahouse dedicated in provide healthy tea made from the finest natural ingredients around the world. We combine our main ingredient, pine needles, from species of pine trees with indigenous plants and herbs from the same area to create local flavors of regions. Through our tea, we hope to bring you the taste of other countries.
