About

Lillian Lin is a Graphic Designer with experience in both web and print. She tackles design problems based on positons such as the creative artist and the analytical thinker. As a creative artist, she seeks out the visually interesting and unusual. The focus is on challenging the viewer to think beyond the ordinary and on providing them with emotive and memorable experiences. As an analytical thinker, she seeks out facts and rationale, embracing structure and legibility in design in order to present content in a simplified way that can be understood with comfortable ease by the public. Rather than viewing these aspects as contradictions, she believes in flexible thinking, treating every project differently based on its specific needs and goals.
