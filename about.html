About

Hi, I'm Lillian Lin, a designer in New York City with experience in both web, digital and print. I like to balance myself between being a creative artist and an analytical thinker when solving design problems. As an artist, it's important to challenge and engage viewers to think beyond the ordinary and to provide them with emotive and memorable visual experiences. As an analyst, I seek facts and rationale, embracing structure and legibility in design to present content that can be understood intuitively. Instead of viewing these aspects to these two sides as contradictions, I believe in flexible thinking. After all, everything is situational.
