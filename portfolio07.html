A Chinese Creation Myth

An ancient Chinese myth is converted into a non-linear interactive web storybook. 15 hand-drawn illustrations in sketched-style is spanned across the entire background to create an immersive experience for the viewer. Focus is placed on hover effects, which creates “surprises” on each page.
